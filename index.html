FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Q: Already added my book/filled out the form and book isnt on here?

A: Sometimes books take a little while before being added either your book isn't the top of the review list or it just hasn't been looked at recently.

Q: How do i find my book?

A: Search for your book with our newly added search menu! Click here to search!

Q: How do i read a book?

A: Our systems are kinda confusing so it a popular question.
1. Go to The Booklist.
2. Choose a series.
3. Click on the book's image.
4. Scroll through the books of the series we have.
5. click on the image of the book you want to read.
6. Scroll down until you see the book use the next and previous buttons to navigate through the pages.

Have a question? email our support team at: support@thebookshelf.ga.
