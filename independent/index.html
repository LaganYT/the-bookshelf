The Bookshelf and independency

The Bookshelf has now become more independent by using its own cdn instead of being powered by glitch. We have almost completly removed ourselves from their platform. The Bookshelf's objective is to give everyone the possibility of reading and writing their own books. I Logan Latham am proud to announce that I am the official CEO of The Bookshelf. You may be asking yourself why should i care? is something changing that will make it harder to get books? To answer that question no. I will not be having any paywalls or limits to books. But i will be improving The Bookshelf for your experience. Some thing that might change soon is:
1. I will be adding more books and more compatibility with more devices such as iphone, mac, and linux.
2. I might make it so you can apply to be a developer for The Bookshelf and help develop new ideas.
If you have any suggestions please click here to suggest them to me and my future crew!
